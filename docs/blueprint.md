# **App Name**: ProdCost Pro

## Core Features:

- Inventory Management: Manage raw material inventory with fields for name, unit of measure, and unit cost.
- Recipe Definition: Define product recipes by specifying ingredients and quantities. Calculates total raw material cost automatically.
- Labor Costing: Calculate labor costs based on processes, time, number of operators, and hourly rates.
- Overhead Allocation: Allocate overhead costs to production based on percentages and generate overhead rates.
- Cost Consolidation: Consolidate raw material, labor, and overhead costs to calculate the total production cost per batch and per unit.
- Reporting: Generate and export reports of production costs, unit costs, and overhead rates in PDF or CSV format.
- Automated Cost Recalculation: Automatically recalculates total costs across all products whenever an ingredient cost, salary, or overhead percentage is updated.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep blue (#3F51B5) to convey professionalism and reliability, fitting for a financial application.
- Background color: Light gray (#F5F5F5), providing a clean and neutral backdrop for data-heavy interfaces.
- Accent color: Soft teal (#4CAF50) used for key actions and highlights, providing a positive and encouraging feel.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for clear, readable text and a modern interface.
- Use minimalist icons to represent different inventory items, processes, and cost components.
- Implement a clear, tabular layout for displaying cost data, ensuring ease of review and analysis.
- Use subtle transitions and animations to indicate data updates and changes, improving user understanding.
